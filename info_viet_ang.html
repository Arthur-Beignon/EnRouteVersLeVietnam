General information of vietnam

Why this Flag ?

Vietnam, located in Southeast Asia, is a fascinating country characterized by its geographical diversity, rich history and culture. With a population of about 96 million, it is one of the most densely populated countries in the region.

Vietnam covers an area of approximately 331,210 square kilometres, offering a variety of landscapes ranging from mountains to coastal plains to rice terraces.

Vietnam’s geography is marked by its mountain ranges, including the Annamites Mountains to the west and the Tonkinese Mountains to the north.

The country is also famous for its spectacular coastal regions, including Halong Bay, home to thousands of karst islands. The Mekong Delta in the south is a vast network of rivers, swamps and rice fields that supports agriculture and rural life.

Economic Information

The Vietnamese economy is constantly expanding and has grown remarkably in recent decades.

Vietnam has managed to balance its socialist heritage with market-oriented economic reforms, which has led to a booming economy. The manufacturing industry, particularly the production of clothing, electronics and agricultural products, plays an essential role in the country’s economy.

In addition, Vietnam has become a key player in the global supply chain, attracting many international companies through an affordable and skilled workforce.

Tourism is constantly growing, contributing significantly to the Vietnamese economy.
